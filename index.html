Whitepaper

TANDANG is a decentralized journalism protocol designed to realign incentives in media. Instead of relying on centralized editorial control, TANDANG introduces a market-driven system where information is priced dynamically based on credibility, engagement, and collective validation. Each story becomes a living asset: - It gains value when trusted and shared - It loses value when ignored or challenged The protocol leverages AI-assisted scoring, community participation, and economic feedback loops to surface higher-quality narratives over time. Goal: Transform journalism into a transparent, incentive-aligned ecosystem.

How It Works

1. Writers submit stories into the open network.
2. AI evaluates structure, depth, and clarity of information.
3. A market value is assigned based on perceived quality.
4. Community interaction reinforces or weakens narratives.
5. High-value stories rise, low-value narratives fade.

The system does not decide truth — it allows truth to emerge through transparent economic signals.

How To Use

• Submit a story using the form above.
• Observe how the AI assigns initial value.
• Track trending narratives in real-time.
• Build reputation as a journalist through consistency.
• Analyze market behavior to identify signal vs noise.

Tip: High-quality, concise, and structured stories tend to perform better in the system.
