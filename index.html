About

TandangNews is an experimental onchain journalism layer where citizens become the first source of truth. Each story is timestamped, hashed, and recorded — forming a permanent record before narratives are shaped.

How it works

1. Submit a real-world story
2. AI verifies signals & consistency
3. Story is hashed & recorded onchain
4. Media or collectors acquire the story

We prioritize anonymity. Submissions are stored as hashes and may be recorded onchain. No personal identity is required. By submitting, you acknowledge that content may become immutable and publicly accessible.
